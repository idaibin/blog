When I write a signal, I try to answer four questions:

- What actually changed
- Why it is worth keeping
- Who should pay attention
- What to do with it next

Keep it short. A good signal helps the reader decide whether it deserves attention today.

If there is a source, write it directly as `Source: <a href="..." target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Source Name</a>` and do not split out a separate URL line.
