When a signal feels important, process it like this:

1. Capture the change in one sentence.
2. Decide whether it affects your work.
3. Rewrite it into a repeatable step.
4. Save the step as a workflow note or prompt.

The goal is not to store news. The goal is to turn a useful update into a habit you can reuse.
